ABOUT

Fauxres is a content creator and gamer who has been playing video games professionally since early 2015. Fauxres has created content ever since he was young but has been producing content on a more consistent basis since 2022. Fauxres has competed at the highest level across numerous titles from Call of Duty to Overwatch.

Fauxres has played Destiny professionally for over 10 years, earning over $27,000 in earnings from Destiny Trials of Osiris. As a member of the Destiny team “GodSquad I” the team has over 30 Tournament wins across the Destiny franchise. He has been featured as a creator by Bungie after raising over $18000 for the Game2Give Charity hosted by the Bungie Foundation. As of 2025 Fauxres has retired from professional Destiny and is looking for a new challenge within the Call of Duty franchise.
